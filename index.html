Welcome to the in2solution wiki!

Ingenious Solutions is a offshore outsourcing company. The founder of this company himself is a developer. This company has been formed to serve the world with modern information technologies. For recent years they are engaged in developing different business software most of these will be integrate with a ERP. The ERP is based on a open source framework. So its development is rapid but easy to adopt.

Products:

  1. A modern ERP (Sales, Purchase, Procurement, Manufacturing, Production, Payroll, Banking, Accounting and Ledger )
  2. Two Accounting Software (Lite and Pro)
  3. Point of Sales solution
  4. Website Development and Design
  5. Domain Registration and Hosting

  7. IT consultancy
  8. Training
